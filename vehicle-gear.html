Vehicle Gear and Holder Design

About

This project aimed to engineer a high-precision gear and holder system for a snowmobile blade, blending iterative simulation and rapid prototyping to push design optimization to new heights.

Task/Action

  • Orchestrated project management within tight constraints, designing over 100 components via SolidWorks with detailed 2D drawings and dimensioning.
  • Applied Design for Manufacturability (DFM) principles, cutting production costs by 10%.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams to enhance blade assembly consistency, reducing failure rates by 20% and boosting throughput by 15% using FEA simulation and failure mode analysis.
  • Evaluated safety, environmental, and legal impacts to ensure a holistic solution.

Result

Delivered a fully functional blade system with 99.9% dimensional accuracy—praised as “one of the most accurate designs” by peers—redefining reliability for extreme conditions.

Tech Stack: SolidWorks, FEA Simulation, DFM Principles
