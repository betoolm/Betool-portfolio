About Me

I am Betool, an engineer exploring the disciplines of design, innovation, and impact. Every project is part of a bigger journey, a pursuit to understand, to build, and to push the limits of what engineering principles can create. When I’m not neck deep in research, I’m fighting... literally. I am a competitive athlete in Muay Thai, where focus, strategy, and discipline are tested with every punch.

Engineering was never just equations and CAD screens for me. It was a challenge, a competition, a battleground of ideas. The same instincts that drive me in the ring, focus, resilience, and relentless problem solving are the instincts I bring into every project I touch. Whether I am designing systems from scratch, prototyping devices that demand both speed and precision, or stress testing ideas until they break, I build to challenge the limits of engineering, I build with purpose. I build to disrupt.

I believe the best ideas aren’t easy. They demand fight. They demand someone willing to step into the arena, take the hits, and engineer something better.

Innovation is my craft. Ambition is my engine. And creativity is the spark that keeps me moving forward. If you’re ready to build bold ideas, I’m already in your corner.
